We support students in tutoring languages at all levels:

Years 1 to 2 (5-7 years old) / Years 3 to 6 (7-11 years old) /Years 7 to 9 (11-14 years old). 
It is indispensable to learn a new language and the youngest you learn a language the better it is assimilated phonetically and it will prepare you better for your GSCE and future professional prospects. We incorporate basic learning, writing and memory skills into our lessons.

From GCSE to A-Levels: We cover many languages and our tutorials focus around the syllabus and techniques to achieve the highest grades

University: We offer tuition in specific languages requested for those sitting entrance exams. We have experienced tutors are at hand to facilitate your progress up to the level required.

Beginners, Intermediate, Advanced :  We assess the student through tests and we progressively create a programme together to meet students’ goals.
At the beginner level we help you to build up your vocabulary, the phonetics and the grammar.  At the intermediate level you should be able to have a conversation, have a good knowledge of the tenses and finally at the advance level we expect you to have very good communication skills, debate in the language, possess good syntax and extend your vocabulary.

Languages for Business: With globalisation and the economic climate of today it is vital to be proficient in languages. We teach languages in all areas of businesses whether it is for medical, law and other sectors of activities we have a wide range of experts experienced in teaching languages for businesses.
We also cater Languages for Holidays(booking a hotel, ordering in a restaurant) and Languages for Pleasure:  It is fun to learn the specific language in your area of hobbies(Arts, Photography). Not only you will progress faster but you will appreciate other cultures and can take the language further.
