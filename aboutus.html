Sharplingo is a unique personalised flexible agency which provides teachers who are native or bilingual speakers and who have been selected for their passion for teaching languages. Their teaching method is assessed to fit our standard of quality. Being native speakers they can pay particular attention to your oral language skills.

We offer tailored tutoring to the needs of the students using proven learning strategies. We believe any student can achieve top marks with the right tutors. We assess the students, their weaknesses and strengths and we work along with their learning style by focusing on the results they desire. We offer constant support and feedback to students in order to achieve the maximum for their goals we set together.

We provide translations for many languages. We carry out translations in Technical, Medical, Legal, Financial and many other fields. All our translators are highly qualified professionals. We will perform most types of translation provided you give us clear guidelines and a reasonable deadline, so if you need a translated document, do not hesitate to contact us for a quotation.

We do proof-reading: We finish the work for you and you can have the confidence to have a well presented document in all languages. We consider each document independently and you can be assured of complete confidentiality. You can send the document by post, e-mail. Please send us a message, stating the nature of the document, the number of words and a deadline.
